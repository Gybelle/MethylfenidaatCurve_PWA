# Rilatine Curve — PWA

A simple progressive web app that shows the effect curve of your Ritalin (methylphenidate IR), including the rebound. You can enter your intake time via "Take now" or via the time field. The app remembers your choice for the rest of the day and shows an orange "Now" marker on the curve."

## How to install on an iOS device
1. Go to https://gybelle.github.io/MethylfenidaatCurve_PWA/ in Safari (not Chrome)
2. Tap the 'Share'- icon at the bottom of your screen
3. Scroll down and choose "Add to Home Screen"
